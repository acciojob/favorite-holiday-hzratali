Santorini, Greece

A breathtaking island in the Aegean Sea, Santorini is known for its stunning sunsets, white-washed buildings, and crystal-clear waters.

Top 5 Attractions:

  1. Red Beach - Marvel at the unique red cliffs and clear waters.
  2. Caldera - Enjoy panoramic views of the volcanic crater and surrounding islands.
  3. Oia Village - Explore the charming streets and iconic blue-domed churches.
  4. Akrotiri Archaeological Site - Discover the ancient Minoan city buried beneath volcanic ash.
  5. Sunset in Fira - Witness one of the most beautiful sunsets in the world.

Must-Try Foods:

  • Fava Santorinis - A traditional Greek dish made from yellow split peas.
  • Tomatokeftedes - Delicious tomato fritters seasoned with herbs and spices.
  • Gyros - A popular Greek street food consisting of meat, onions, tomatoes, and tzatziki sauce wrapped in pita bread.
  • Santorini Salad - A refreshing salad made with cherry tomatoes, cucumber, capers, olives, and feta cheese.
  • Baklava - A sweet pastry made of layers of filo dough, chopped nuts, and honey.
